Services

Short-Form video editing

I will give life to your raw footage, creating short-format videos for social media with an editing style designed to attract more audience or leads to your accounts.

Every short format video could include:

-General cuts and phase.

-Colorimetry.

-Animations & VFX.
-Audio correction & leveling.
-SFX.
-Subtitles.

Script-writing & Content ideation

Are you not sure what to talk about on your account? Or what would it work to attract more people?


Don't worry! I will give you multiple content ideas based on your experience and what you feel comfortable sharing. After that, I will take care of creating multiple scripts, so you just need to focus on recording yourself for a few moments each week.


(And yes! We will adjust each content bundle after analyzing your account KPIs in order to maximize growth)

social media management & lead generation

If you don't have time to handle everything, I can also help!


I will create a tailored content calendar designed to ensure consistency across platforms and prevent missed posts. You won't need to worry about anything more than approving the content before it goes out!


And if you're a business and want to leverage social media to generate more leads, I've got you covered.


I will build automation with third-party apps to gather as many leads as possible so you can contact them and increase your business revenue.
